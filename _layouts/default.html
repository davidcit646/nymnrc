About

We at NYMNRC have been dealing in diamonds for the better part of a century. Founded in 1923, we have been helping clients succesfully buy and sell diamonds and have completed many different trade deals. We have experience both locally, here in the United States, but also abroad in countries like India, Isreal, Belgium, and Guinea to name a few. We hope to continue having succesful transactions with our clients, and hope we can strive to exist as a company for another century.
